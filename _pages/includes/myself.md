# 🍑 Personal

**Room78**

I co-founded the Room78 Algorithm Club in 2020 at ZJU's Haining Campus and served as its president until September 2022. We built and maintain our own online judge system, which is used for daily training, practice, and small-scale simulation contests. Room78 also organizes workshops on research and competition experience sharing, as well as algorithm lectures such as "Our Life Dominated by Algorithms" for students on the Haining campus.

**Tuxun**

I also enjoy playing Tuxun, a geography-guessing game similar to GeoGuessr. My [Tuxun profile](https://tuxun.fun/user/807297) is mainly focused on locations in China, and I reached the top 8 in the Fourth Tuxun Intercollegiate Competition.
